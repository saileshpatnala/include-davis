ABOUT #Include

UCD AGGIES
CODING-DESIGNING-MANAGING-EDUCATING
FOR NONPROFITS 100% FREE

#Include - Include the Community is a student-run organization that was founded by UC Davis computer science students during the summer of 2016.

The club was founded because more and more non-profits were in need of a new or updated website, computer science students wanted more hands-on, practical experience, and non-computer science students wanted the chance to learn coding and experience the web development field. The solution? A close-knit club that benefits all parties.

GOAL: Our main goal is to aid non-profits by providing technology assistance through website setup, redesigning, and maintanence. Whether you be a researcher, professor, or are part of a local charity, we're glad to help anyone and in anyway we can with our webdev skills.

We want to give back to our community. One website at a time.

HOW IT WORKS

The Meeting

After you contact us, we'll set up a in-person/phone/skype meeting - whichever works best. We'll discuss deadlines, availability, your organzation or product, and any concerns you may have.

The Ideas

After our meeting, we'll get started setting up ASAP. We'll continue communication through emails so we can have the right info for your website as well as your input and preferences on the design of the new website.

The Finished Website

When we're done, we'll "publish" the website and hand the source code to you. If you like, we can even show you some basic HTML so you can easily maintain your new website!

SERVICES PROVIDED

WEBSITE SETUP & CUSTOMIZATION

Lacking a website for your organization or product? We can set one up for you! We'll be sure to get your input on customization and design and set up a beautiful website.

WEBSITE REDESIGN

If your website is outdated our team is prepared to redesign its' looks. Whether you request simple changes or a complete overhaul, wer'e here to help!

WEBSITE MAINTENANCE & UPKEEP

If your organization or product's info has changed and you're not sure how to make necessary website info updates then we can help you with that. We can also show you how you can easily maintain your website.

DIGITAL MARKETING

The improvement of your website can have a major impact on marketing. Additionally, we can help showcause your organization. Contact for more details.

JOIN #Include

Why Join?

Join us at #Include. We're gonna be honest with you. There are many benefits for joining :) There's pros for everyone. Where should we start?

Whether you be a computer science, chemical engineering, psychology, animal science major - you name it, you can become an integral part of this code-based club. We seek to help through code and creativity and have fun while at it! Whether you be a freshman straight out from high school, a fifth year, a transfer student, we're certain you will contribute!

Whether you're learning or teaching code in #Include, you'll start getting hands-on experience in front-end development, meet like-minded people in our close-knit community, volunteer by coding or managing, gain practical experience, and aid the community - all in one go.

Not confident of your coding skills or don't know how to code? It's okay! We're a learning team - we can show you how. You don't have to be a cs major to code. You will learn how to create websites using HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap. If you have any questions, be sure to contact us -we'd love to answer any questions you have.
